About Party Pixels

Party Pixels is an indie web playground: small games, mini tools, and experimental “web toys” built for fun and quick prototypes.

What you’ll find here

  • Simple browser games (quick sessions, mobile-friendly)
  • Generators and playful interactive tools
  • Prototype features that may change or break

Project status

This site is a work in progress. Some toys are rough, some are stable, and some are experiments.
