The Game

core rules

War On The Block is a fast, aggressive card duel built around units, action cards, and neighborhood drama. Reduce your opponent’s HP from 15 to 0 before they do the same to you.

2–6 players
Works as tight 1v1 duels or chaotic multiplayer block wars.
20-card deck
Units and action cards. Small deck. Big swings.
One unit limit
Normally you can control only one unit at a time. Every decision matters.
Shame mechanic
Units marked with Shame lose power and abilities next turn. Humiliation is a weapon.

How It Plays

fast overview
Start
Each player begins with 15 HP and 5 cards.
Your turn
Draw a card. Play one card (unit or action). Resolve effects.
Combat
If both players control a unit, they deal damage equal to their power. Excess damage hits the player directly.
Win
Reduce an opponent to 0 HP to win the block.

Optional quick mode: start at 10 HP and remove the one-unit limit for pure chaos.

What is War On The Block?

short pitch

War On The Block is a battle card game set in a gritty cartoon universe about neighborhood beef, stupid plans, and nights that spiral out of control. It exists in two formats: a digital chaos brawler and a fast, mean card game. Same cast, same vibe — different ways to start trouble.
