About this test

Are you harmless or are you the issue?

Am I the Problem? is a funny personality quiz that gives you a fake-serious verdict on your drama level in friendships, relationships, work chats, and random online nonsense.

It is not science. It is judgment. Extremely stylish judgment.

How it works

Answer 10 multiple-choice questions. Each answer adds to your chaos score. The higher your score, the more likely it is that people have at least one story about you.

Who this is for

People with group chats, exes, strong opinions, bad timing, or a suspicious history of hearing “bro, relax.”

Why people love tests like this

Because everyone wants confirmation that they are either perfectly innocent or a charismatic disaster with great comedic timing.
