Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Party Pixels (“we”) collects and uses information when you use our website.

Data we collect

  • Account data (if you log in): email and basic profile info (e.g., display name, bio).
  • Gameplay data (optional): scores and basic metadata related to web toys.
  • Technical data: standard logs (IP address, user agent) may be processed by hosting and third-party services.

Cookies and similar technologies

We and our partners may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Maintain sessions (login)
  • Measure usage and performance
  • Show ads (if/when enabled)

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We may display ads served by Google (Google AdSense). Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites.

  • You can learn more about Google’s advertising policies and controls in Google’s own resources.
  • You can manage ad personalization settings in your Google account settings.

Third-party services

We use third-party services to run the site, including hosting and authentication/database providers. These services may process limited data necessary to provide their functionality.

Data storage

  • Account and score data may be stored in our database provider (Supabase).
  • Hosting and delivery is provided by Vercel.

Your choices

  • If you do not want your scores saved, you can use the site without logging in.
  • You may request deletion of your profile data by contacting us (see Contact page).

Children

Party Pixels is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly.
