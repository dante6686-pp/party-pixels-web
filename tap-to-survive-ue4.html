Core loop

What the game is about

  • Your life constantly drains as you move through dangerous zones.
  • Tapping (or clicking/pressing) keeps you alive and powers up your ride.
  • Collect resources to unlock upgrades, buffs and visual chaos.
  • Short, intense runs designed for “one more try” sessions.

This page is a landing for the UE4 version. The small browser prototype on this site is a separate toy that lets people taste the core panic mechanic instantly.
